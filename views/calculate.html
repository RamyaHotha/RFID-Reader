About Body Mass Index (BMI)
The Body Mass Index (BMI), is a statistical measure of body weight based on a person's weight and height. BMI is a widely used measure to find out whether you're a healthy weight for your height. A BMI of 18.5 to 25 is considered healthy for most adults. ×
